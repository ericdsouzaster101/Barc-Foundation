A little about us

We are barc ie: Belgaum Animal Rescue and Care. We dedicate our lives for the voiceless. We rescue wounded animals and the animals which are abandoned. We have a shelter where we treat injured animals. We also help pets get adopted so that they get a loving family. We also raise our voice against animal cruelty and take legal measures.

We do amazing things for the voiceless

ADOPTION

Adopt dont shop.Thats right! We help stray animals get adopted and give them a happy life ahead with a loving family. Every adoption procedure is done with thorough study and genuinity.

RESCUE OPERATIONS

Animals meet with accidents with vehicles all the time. Some suffocate to death but the ones which are alive we do our best to reach out to them and give them the necessary treatment.

ANIMAL RIGHTS ACTIVISTS

We reach out to the common people about the awareness on animal rights. We take necessary legal actions and procedures accordingly with respective cases.
